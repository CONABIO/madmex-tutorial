Spatial Segmentation

The Madmex case

Madmex uses Berkeley image segmentation (BIS)

  • Groups pixels according to their spectral similarity and spatial proximity
  • Proprietary software

Advantages of spatial segmentation

  • More realistic representation of the Landscaope
  • Reduce noise of individual pixels
  • Enables working with minimum mapping unit
